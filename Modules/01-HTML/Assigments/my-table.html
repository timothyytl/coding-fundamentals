TYPES OF COFFEE BEANS

TYPE DESCRIPTION
Arabica
  • the most popular
  • prefered due to taste (sweeter, complexed flavor)
  • typically used for black coffee
  • lower caffeine content as compared to Robusta
Robusta
  • cheaper and stronger
  • bitter in flavour
  • typically used for espresso drinks and instant coffee mixes
  • high in caffeine

TYPES OF COFFEE DRINKS

TYPE PREPARATION
Black Ground coffee beans steeped in hot water, served warm.
Latte 1 shot of espresso and steamed milk with a touch of foam.
Cappuccino Latte made with more foam than steamed milk, often with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon on top.
Americano 1 espresso shot diluted in hot water.
Espresso An espresso shot can be served solo. Also used as the foundation of most coffee drinks.
Macchiato Espresso with small amount of foam on top.
Mocha Chocolate espresso with steamed milk and foam.
Ristretto Espresso with lesser hot water which creates a sweeter flavor compared to the bitter taste of traditional espresso.
Flat white Aussie-made espresso with steamed milk without the foam or chocolate sprinkle.
